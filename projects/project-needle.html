Double-Arch Needle Insertion Robot

Liang, Z.; Hashem, R.; Stilli, A. et al.

Short summary: a 5-DoF MR-safe double-arch needle insertion robot for abdominal percutaneous interventions with a scissor-folding mechanism.

  • Problem: Accurate and safe needle insertion inside MR environments for liver/kidney interventions.
  • Approach: Double-arch kinematic architecture with MR-safe materials and pneumatic actuation.
  • Outcome: Prototype accepted to EMBC / IROS and demonstrates improved insertion control.
