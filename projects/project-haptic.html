Haptic Glove for Grip Training

Lei, Y.; Hashem, R. et al.

Short summary: a modular glove that provides proprioceptive haptic feedback for grip-force regulation training.

  • Problem: Training tactile/proprioceptive skills in rehabilitation and skill transfer.
  • Approach: Modular actuators with multi-frequency output to provide graded force feedback.
  • Outcome: Prototype validated in lab; manuscript submitted to RoboSoft.
