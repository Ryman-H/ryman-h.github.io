SoRSS — Soft Robotic Gastric Simulator

Hashem, R. et al. — Soft Robotics (2022)

SoRSS is a soft robotic simulator designed to replicate stomach anatomy and antral motility for device testing and control research.

  • Problem: No controllable platform for studying gastric motility.
  • Approach: Ring-shaped soft actuators with closed-loop control.
  • Outcome: Published soft robotic gastric simulator used in control studies.
  • Domain: Soft robotics, physiological modelling, device validation.
