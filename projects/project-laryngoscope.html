Sensorised Laryngoscope & MR Holographic Feedback

Li, J.; Hashem, R.; He, L. et al.

Short summary: mixed-reality holographic feedback coupled with a sensorised laryngoscope to improve paediatric intubation training.

  • Problem: Improve training fidelity and outcomes in paediatric airway management.
  • Approach: Integrate mixed reality overlays with a sensorised laryngoscope and provide real-time holographic guidance.
  • Outcome: Promising improvements in training performance; manuscript submitted.
