MR-safe Pneumatic Stepper Motor

Liang, Z.; Hashem, R. et al.

Short summary: a low-cost, MR-safe pneumatic stepper motor for use inside magnetic resonance imaging environments.

  • Problem: Actuation solutions for surgical robotics that are safe and functional inside MRI scanners.
  • Approach: Design an MR-compatible pneumatic stepping mechanism and characterise force/precision.
  • Outcome: Prototype demonstrates motion control with minimal imaging artefact; submitted to IEEE Sensors.
