Biomimetic Gastric Simulator

Kazemi, S.; Hashem, R. et al.

Short summary: a biomimetic simulator that reproduces antral contraction waves for testing control strategies and validating soft actuator behaviour.

  • Problem: Need for realistic testing environments for soft actuators and control algorithms.
  • Approach: Constructed a soft ring-shaped actuator system and implemented closed-loop control to reproduce physiological contraction patterns.
  • Outcome: Demonstrated effective closed-loop control of soft actuators and published experimental results.
  • Materials & methods: Pneumatic soft actuators, motion capture / pressure sensing, in-vitro experiments.
