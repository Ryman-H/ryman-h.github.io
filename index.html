Soft & Medical Robotics · Translational Research

About

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the University of Oxford working at the intersection of soft robotics, medical phantoms, and translational research. My work focuses on developing anatomically realistic systems for training, device validation, and clinical applications.

I design and build soft robotic platforms that replicate physiological behaviour, combining compliant actuation, embedded sensing, and experimental validation. My research spans benchtop testing through to clinical-facing translational studies.

I collaborate closely with clinicians and engineers and supervise undergraduate and postgraduate projects. I am currently open to Lecturer and Assistant Professor opportunities.

Research Interests

Soft robotics, medical phantoms, sensorisation, haptics, and clinical validation.
